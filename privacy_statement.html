Netpy Privacy Statement

This privacy statement is effective as of November 22, 2024. Please note that this privacy statement will regularly be updated to reflect any changes in the way we handle your personal data or any changes in applicable laws.

Unless stated otherwise, this privacy statement applies to all of NetPy Technologies externally facing applications, services, tools, websites, and other data processing activities where NetPy Technologies is acting as a data controller. This page and its sub-pages provide comprehensive information about how NetPy Technologies protects the personal data we process and control relating to you and the rights you have regarding the processing of your personal data.

Information We Collect


We collect various types of personal information to provide our IT solutions, robotics learning, mentorship programs, and internship opportunities. This may include:

  • Contact information (e.g., name, email address)
  • Professional information (e.g., job title, company)
  • Educational background
  • Interaction data (e.g., usage of our services)

Use of Personal Data


NetPy Technologies uses your personal data for several purposes, including:

  • Providing and managing our services
  • Communicating with you regarding your inquiries or requests
  • Improving our services based on user feedback
  • Sending promotional materials related to our offerings

Data Sharing Practices


We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services. We ensure that these parties are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes specified by us.

User Rights


You have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions

Security Measures


NetPy Technologies implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Cookies Policy


We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience on our website. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to This Privacy Statement


We may update this privacy statement from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy statement on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Information


If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy statement or our data practices, please contact us at support@netpy.in.

This privacy statement is designed to inform you about how we handle your personal information at NetPy Technologies and ensure transparency in our operations.
